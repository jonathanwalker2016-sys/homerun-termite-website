Common Questions

FAQ

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How much does a home pest or termite inspection cost? ▾
Residential home inspections for homeowners are completely FREE. Just call or text to schedule. (Note: this does not apply to WDI inspections for real estate transactions.)
What do WDI inspections cost for real estate? ▾
WDI (Wood Destroying Insect) inspections for real estate are $90 plus tax. We provide detailed, honest reports with fast turnaround — perfect for mortgage and refinance timelines.
Can you warranty my home against termites? ▾
Yes! We offer affordable annual termite warranty plans. This requires an initial inspection confirming your home is currently termite-free. A history of past treatments is also helpful. Call us to get started.
What if I have fleas, roaches, bed bugs, or brown recluse spiders? ▾
We have targeted treatments for each of these. We understand how frustrating — and embarrassing — these situations can be. We'll walk you through the process step by step and partner with you to eliminate and exclude them from your home.
Do you service commercial properties, restaurants, or offices? ▾
Absolutely. We work with hospitals, clinics, office buildings, rental properties, and more. We'll create a completely customized pest management plan to fit your operation's specific needs and schedule.
What areas do you serve? ▾
We serve Topeka, Holton, Hoyt, Meriden, Mayetta, Lawrence, Tecumseh, Carbondale, Silver Lake, Valley Falls, Rossville, St. Marys, and surrounding areas of Northeast Kansas.
